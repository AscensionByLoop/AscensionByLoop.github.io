Ascension

Ascension is an online multiplayer FPS game with RTS elements.

Set aboard an abandonded curise ship.

Players will play a 5 round deathmatch to obtain the most kills while traversing an abandoned cruise ship. Our three maps: Pool party, Deck A and Social Engine let players explore and fight in a death battle in a ship environment.

First Person Shooter

Players are given a variety of styalised guns such as a harpoon gun and a water pistol. Allowing you to battle your opponenets in a fun and intersting way.

Real Time Strategy

Those skilled enough will be chosen as the Havoc player. One player on each time will be given godly abilites to change the flow of the game and a top down view of the map.

The Havoc Player

At the end of each round a Havoc player is chosen and given godly abilities. These godly abilities can either help or hinder others. By dropping bombs or health packs Havoc players can change the flow of the game and help turn the tides in their teams favor.

Change the Game

With a variety of objects to spawn you can choose anyway in which YOU can affect the game.

See the Map

With a top down view of the map and the ability to look at different layers you can see whats going on at all times.

Follow the players

You can keep on top the players by following them with the simple click of a button.

The Maps

There are three maps that allow you to explore the vast ocean curise ship the game is set upon. Pool party, Deck A and Social/Engine give you a variety of shp like environments to explore.

Deck A

The leisure centre of the ship. Passengers can enjoy their times watching films in the cinema, playing poker or cards and drinking at the bar.

Pool party

On the top deck of our Cruise ship passangers can enjoy their time playing in the pool and drinking at the bar. Our viewing tower will also give passangers a beautiful view of the ocean.

Social Engine

Another deck for passangers to kick back and relax with drinking and a childs play area. The lower levels are for employees only!
